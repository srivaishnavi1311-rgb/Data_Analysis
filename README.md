# Data_Analysis
Developed an interactive Sales Analysis Dashboard in Microsoft Excel using Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, and Slicers to analyze sales trends, customer behavior, regional performance, and sales channel contribution for data-driven business insights.

# Project Overview

The Store Sales Analysis Dashboard is an interactive business intelligence solution developed in Microsoft Excel to analyze sales performance and customer purchasing behavior. The dashboard consolidates raw transactional data into meaningful visual insights using Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, Slicers, and Excel data analysis techniques.

Designed to support data-driven decision-making, the dashboard enables users to explore sales trends, customer demographics, regional performance, order fulfillment, and channel-wise contributions through dynamic filtering and interactive visualizations.

# Business Objective

The objective of this project is to transform raw sales data into actionable business insights by identifying key sales trends, customer preferences, and operational performance. The dashboard assists stakeholders in evaluating business performance and making informed strategic decisions related to sales, marketing, and inventory management.

Dashboard Features

The dashboard provides an interactive overview of business performance through the following visualizations:

# Monthly Orders vs Sales
Compares monthly sales revenue with order volume.
Identifies seasonal trends and peak business periods.
# Gender-wise Sales Analysis
Evaluates sales contribution by male and female customers.
Supports customer segmentation and targeted marketing strategies.
# Order Status Analysis
Presents the distribution of Delivered, Cancelled, Returned, and Refunded orders.
Measures order fulfillment efficiency and operational performance.
# Top 5 States by Sales
Highlights the highest revenue-generating states.
Assists in identifying key regional markets.
# Age Group vs Gender Analysis
Analyzes purchasing behavior across Adult, Senior, and Teenager customer segments.
Provides insights into customer demographics.
# Sales Channel Performance
Compares order contribution across multiple sales channels including Amazon, Flipkart, Myntra, Ajio, Meesho, Nalli, and others.
Identifies the most effective sales platforms.
Interactive Filters

The dashboard includes dynamic slicers that allow users to perform customized analysis based on:

Month
Sales Channel
Product Category

These filters update all visualizations simultaneously, enabling efficient and flexible data exploration.

# Key Insights
Female customers account for a significantly larger share of total sales compared to male customers.
Adult customers represent the largest purchasing segment across all demographics.
Monthly sales exhibit noticeable fluctuations, indicating seasonal purchasing patterns.
Successfully delivered orders constitute the majority of total orders, reflecting strong operational performance.
Maharashtra, Karnataka, Uttar Pradesh, Telangana, and Tamil Nadu are the leading revenue-generating states.
Amazon is the highest-performing sales channel in terms of order contribution.
Interactive filtering enables detailed analysis across different business dimensions.

This project demonstrates the effective use of Microsoft Excel as a business intelligence tool for retail sales analysis. By integrating interactive dashboards with data visualization techniques, the solution converts complex transactional data into meaningful business insights, enabling faster analysis and informed decision-making.
